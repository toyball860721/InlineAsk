🦞 InlineAsk - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

TL;DR: InlineAsk does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device and goes directly to your chosen AI API.

1. Information Collection

InlineAsk does NOT collect any user data.

We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Store your conversations
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share data with third parties

2. Data Storage

InlineAsk stores the following information locally on your device only using Chrome's storage API:

  • API Endpoint URL - The URL of your chosen AI service
  • API Key - Your authentication key (stored encrypted by Chrome)
  • Model Name - Your preferred AI model

This data never leaves your device except when you explicitly send requests to your configured API endpoint.

3. Data Transmission

When you use InlineAsk to ask questions:

  • Your selected text and questions are sent directly to your configured API endpoint
  • Data flows: Your Browser → Your Chosen API (OpenAI, Claude, Qwen, etc.)
  • InlineAsk acts as a pass-through and does not intercept, store, or process your data
  • We have no servers - all processing happens on your device

4. Third-Party Services

InlineAsk does not use any third-party services for analytics, tracking, or data collection.

However, when you configure an AI API (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Alibaba, etc.), your data is subject to that service's privacy policy:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Alibaba Cloud Privacy Policy

For local APIs (Ollama, LM Studio), data never leaves your device.

5. Permissions Explanation

InlineAsk requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage - To save your API configuration locally
  • tabs - To inject the text selection interface into web pages
  • scripting - To enable text selection detection
  • <all_urls> - To work on any webpage you visit

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and not for data collection.

6. Data Security

  • Your API keys are stored using Chrome's secure storage API
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is logged or transmitted to InlineAsk developers

7. Children's Privacy

InlineAsk does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
